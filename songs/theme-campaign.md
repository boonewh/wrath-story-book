⚔️ "We Do Not Break" — Anthemic Symphonic Crusader Metal

The CAMPAIGN THEME — not a per-session song and not a single-character portrait, but the communal battle-hymn of the whole crusade and the four who hold the line. It is sung in "we," never "I." The concept is faith in the foreground and the fight rising up underneath it: the verses are prayer, low and steady with the gods named at the heroes' backs, and the choruses are the army roaring as the fight breaks to the surface. Verse 1 names the four heroes by their four gods — Torag at the hammer, Shelyn at the glaive, Irori at the open fist, Alseta at the door — and a later verse hands the whole crusade to Iomedae, the Inheritor, the radiant sword and the banner a hundred thousand march behind. The Worldwound is a wound torn open in the ground itself, a rift in the world, and the oath is to march north to Drezen and close it — the chorus no longer carries anyone home; it points forward, ending on "till the wound is closed." Hope and iron, defiance without despair — a hymn and a war-cry at once, now full-length. ## Troubleshooting

1. **If it comes out too grimdark/harsh:** Lead the style box with "uplifting triumphant clean vocal, hopeful and defiant, no harsh vocals, no screaming." Cut any word Suno reads as despair; keep "war-march" but pair it with "triumphant."
2. **If it loses the big singable clean chorus:** Keep "big singable clean anthem chorus with gang vocals" at the very front of the style box, and in the lyrics keep the chorus identical every repeat so Suno locks the hook — never vary the chorus wording (it is identical on all three repeats here).
3. **If the orchestra/choir overwhelms the song:** Add "guitars and drums lead the mix, orchestra and choir support underneath" and reduce to "subtle choir hum in verses, choir only swells on choruses."
4. **If the orchestra/choir vanishes:** Add "prominent full mixed choir and sweeping orchestral strings and brass present throughout, especially huge on every chorus," and name the organ swell explicitly.
5. **If it drifts toward a real-world religious/hymnal sound (gospel, chant, sacred choral):** Strip the word "hymn" from the style box and replace with "epic fantasy battle-anthem"; remove "organ," keep "cathedral organ swell" only if it stays metal; add "no gospel, no liturgical chant, fantasy crusade not real-world worship."
6. **To keep the single register (no genre transition):** Repeat "single register, dynamic swells only, no genre change, no tempo change, no key change" at the end of the style box. If Suno tries a soft folk intro that becomes metal, add "full band in from the first bar, no acoustic intro."
7. **If Suno mis-sings "tear":** It is already avoided — the Worldwound is "torn," a "rift," a "wound," a "scar," "ripped open," never the noun "tear." If you edit the lyrics, do NOT reintroduce the word "tear" (Suno sings it as the eye-kind).
