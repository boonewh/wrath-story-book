🎯 Quick Tips for This Build
The inline [Section Tags] in the lyrics are doing most of the heavy lifting now — Suno honors those much more reliably than long style descriptions. The style box just sets the overall palette; the section tags tell Suno when to shift gears.
A few things worth knowing:
The single word "Alone" sitting on its own line after [Held chord, suspended, full orchestra rising] is Suno's cue to pause and slam. If the first generation rushes past it, regenerate — Suno sometimes nails this on attempt two or three even when attempt one missed it.
The bridge is where Suno tends to either shine or fall flat. If the ghostly female voice doesn't come through distinct enough, you can regenerate with [Bridge - male whisper and distant female harmony, call and response] instead. Suno reads "ghostly" inconsistently.
If the final chorus doesn't punch hard enough, add the word EPIC or MASSIVE in caps right before that section tag — Suno responds to that kind of intensity cue.
And finally: generate a few times. Suno is genuinely variable run-to-run on builds like this. The same prompt can give you a flat take and a stunning take back to back. Don't judge the prompt by the first generation alone.
