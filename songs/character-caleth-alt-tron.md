## Troubleshooting

1. **If the orchestra vanishes and it goes thin / pure-synth:** Lead the style box with "full symphonic strings and brass present in every section, layered WITH the synth arpeggios — orchestral-electronic hybrid, not synth alone" and add "cinematic string and brass swells under the chorus." The orchestra is half the sound; it cannot drop out.
2. **If it loses the driving pulse / becomes a slow ballad:** Push "steady driving four-on-the-floor electronic kick throughout, ~150 BPM, relentless pulsing sub-bass and cycling arpeggio as the spine — propulsive, NOT a ballad, NOT a slow build." The forward motion is the engine of the whole track.
3. **If the vocal goes robotic / non-melodic / vocoder-heavy:** Re-state "intense MELODIC HUMAN male lead vocal carrying every line, fully sung — vocoder/robotic texture ONLY as a faint background accent, NEVER on the lead." If a verse still comes back processed, add "clean expressive human lead, no autotune-robot effect on the main voice."
4. **If it drifts off the neon-cinematic mood (too soft, too generic, too party):** Front-load "dark, epic, neon-digital crusader anthem — heroic and propulsive, blue-white arcane glyphs, rain on ruined battlements, blazing dawn. No bright club/party feel." Keep "neon holy war" and "cinematic" near the top.
5. **If Suno tries to swap registers (e.g. quiet electronic verse → metal/rock chorus, or orchestral verse → EDM drop):** This is the failure mode to guard hardest. Re-state "ONE orchestral-electronic register start to finish, dynamic swells ONLY — tense verse to exploding chorus, but the SAME hybrid texture throughout. No genre change, no key change, no tempo change." The chorus should get bigger and add distorted guitars, not become a different song.
