## Troubleshooting

1. **If it goes anthemic/soaring:** Pull "choir hum" down or cut it entirely, and add "restrained, no belting, grave low vocal" to the front of the style box.
2. **If the tempo runs fast:** Lead with "Slow doom tempo, 70 BPM, deliberate" and consider dropping the word "war" — it pushes Suno to speed up.
3. **If the vocal comes back pop/clean/female:** Add "gruff solo male baritone, spoken-edge delivery." The outro "North. Drezen. Staunton dies." should land near-spoken — if Suno sings it melodically, add (spoken) after it in the lyrics box.
