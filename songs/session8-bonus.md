## Troubleshooting

1. **If Suno slows the opening or adds an intro ramp:** Put "fast start, vocals in immediately" at the front of the style box — the under-2:25 target plus 116 BPM is what keeps it from drifting toward ballad pacing.
2. **If the choir doesn't come in on the chorus:** Move "Massive battle-choir" to lead the style box; Suno weights early descriptors more heavily.
3. **If a breakdown appears at the bridge:** Keep "no breakdown, no quiet drop" in the style box — that guard is doing the work. Removing it invites a dynamic dip Suno can't recover from in a sub-2:30 track.
