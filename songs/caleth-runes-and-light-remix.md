## Troubleshooting

- **If it comes back aggressive/screamo:** Push BPM descriptor down to "118-125" temporarily and add "mid-tempo, restrained vocal, spoken-sung swagger" to the front of the style box. Speed tips Suno into screamo — once you get the right vocal tone, bump it back to 138.
- **If the vocal turns into shouty chanting:** Add "smooth melodic phrasing on the chorus hook" to the style box. If the bridge's stacked short lines are triggering it, collapse them to one line temporarily.
- **If it loses the synth hook / sounds like generic rock:** Lead the style box with "instrumental: dominant filtered squelchy analog synth riff" and drop any mention of guitars entirely.
- **If it adds long instrumental intros/outros:** Reinforce "no instrumental padding, under 2:30, single consistent groove throughout" and consider adding "cold open, vocals in by 0:05."
