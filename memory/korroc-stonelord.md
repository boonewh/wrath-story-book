Korroc's paladin archetype is **Stonelord** (Pathfinder). Stone literally runs through his veins. Now reflected in [[characters/korroc.md]] (Class & Build, Interior Voice, and Faith sections). The character file is source of truth; this memory exists to surface the *why* and the *prose application*.

**Why:** Stonelord is a Pathfinder paladin archetype tied to earth-magic, ancestor-stone, and clanhold lineage. It deepens Korroc beyond "smith with a hammer" into "smith whose body remembers the bones of the world."

**How to apply:** Korroc's POV interior voice is still rooted in forge-imagery — that stays canonical and is well-established in chapters 1-3. But Stonelord adds a complementary **stone-metaphor layer** that should start surfacing in his future POV chapters, especially after the Mythic inflection (sessions 10-15) when the README explicitly says his "forge-metaphors become temple-metaphors." Stonelord lets that grow further: stone in him recognizing stone outside him, ancestor-weight in his footfalls, the literal earth answering when he prays. Don't replace the forge — *layer* the stone underneath it.
