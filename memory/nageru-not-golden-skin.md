Nageru's skin is **warm, sun-kissed bronze.** *Not golden.* This is now established in [[characters/nageru.md]] (Appearance section) with an explicit "do not write golden skin" directive.

His Aasimar heritage manifests as **a subtle, almost imperceptible golden *glow* / aura** — separate from skin tone. The glow is real and visible in the right light; the skin underneath it is bronze.

**Eyes:** amber (not "deep gold or amber" — just amber).

**Why this memory exists:** A previous version of [[characters/nageru.md]] described him with "faintly luminous golden skin," which leaked into image generation prompts and prose. The user explicitly corrected this. Both the file and prose should reflect bronze-skin-plus-glow-aura, not gold-skin.

**How to apply:**
- Skin tone in prose: bronze, sun-kissed, dark, warm — **never golden, never luminous as a skin property.**
- The glow is the aura/heritage, not pigment. Treat it as something that *emanates from his presence*, like the heat off a fire — there but distinct from his body.
- Celestial fire flaring along his fists during divine smites (e.g. Chapter 1 dretch fight) is *active* divine manifestation; that's fine and on-canon.
- He has **no facial scars** ("a testament to disciplined parrying"); short, dark brown hair tied back; 6'2"; lean and powerful.
